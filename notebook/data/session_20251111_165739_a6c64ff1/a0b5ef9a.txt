Prevalence of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Rapid Iteration and Model Updates
The report finds that more than 50% of respondents update their AI models at least monthly. This rapid iteration pace points to the dynamic nature of AI deployments and the need to keep models aligned with evolving data and user requirements.

Expanding Use Cases Across Teams
94% of teams utilize large language models (LLMs) for at least two different use cases, with 82% supporting three or more use cases. This multi-functionality trend indicates that LLMs are becoming versatile tools across internal and external applications such as code generation, writing assistance, and customer service.

Emerging Domains and Trends
Audio AI is poised for a significant adoption wave, with 37% planning to incorporate audio capabilities soon. Additionally, 41% of teams engage in fine-tuning models (including LoRA/QLoRA methods), revealing a maturing engineering approach beyond API usage toward customizing AI for specialized tasks.
